ONE-STOP PLATFORM

Build strong communities by introducing lifestyle, cultural and community services-related programs to help expatriates and returnees integrate their lives here in Shanghai.

Offer a direct window at our center for foreigners' work permit application, provide an employment platform that connects job seekers to potential employers and offer a range of professional consultation services in regards to employment, business registration, personal tax policies and pro-bono legal aid to help expatriates develop their careers and resolve work-related issues in Shanghai.

Conduct a wide range of activities such as Exploring Educational Opportunities (EEO) event, Family Days, marriage preparation and counseling courses, parenting classes and mother-to-mother support groups to strengthen family bonding and make Shanghai a successful and loving home away from home.
