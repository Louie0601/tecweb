Mark Franklind Braithwaite

Sweden


Asia-Pacific (APAC) President of Höganäs AB

Mark received his degree in Chemistry back in 1991-1994 and qualified as Chartered Accountant (ACA) in 1998. He then started his career as an auditor with PwC (PricewaterhouseCoopers) and then Merrill Lynch Investment Banking. Thereafter, he joined Höganäs AB, a Swedish engineering company in 2003 in the UK and held various positions. Most recently as group CFO until 2018 when he moved to China as President of Asia-Pacific.

“I have supported TEC at various events including discussion panel and as an Emcee.”
