Tiffany Caoxu

China


Founder & CEO of Tcube

Tiffany received her Honor in Math & Financial Risk Management from the University of Minnesota, followed by completing her master’s degree in Actuarial Science from Columbia University. Currently, Tiffany is the Founder and CEO of Tcube; also, the Co-Founder of Yuanhe Investment Co. She serves as the Deputy Secretary General of US Chapter at Shanghai Overseas Returned Scholars Association (SORSA), as well as the Co-chair of Big10 Alum Associations.



“I got acquainted with TEC in an educational companion project sharing session that I’ve initially invested in. I resonated with TEC's value and was deeply affected by TEC's efforts to Build A Home Away From Home for foreigners for many years. It is always hard to imagine so many activities and large Fairs were organized by a team of only a few full-time staffs. From paying attention to TEC's activities, workshops, to participating in the activities as a volunteer at the Christmas charity gala, helping to promote the TEC mini program... there are many I’d hope to contribute to this community and bring more strategic partners on board to help TEC build charitable activities together. I look forward for the future!”
