Jiayi Du

China


College Teacher of Shanghai International Business and Economics

Jiayi is a college teacher from Shanghai International Business and Economics. Her research and academic interests focus on consumer behavior and social media marketing. She also works as a commenter for China Global Television Network and a freelance writer for Shanghai Morning Post. During her leisure time, she devoted herself to social works. She established NYU Alumni Club in Shanghai in 2013 and has served more than 1000 NYU alumni in Shanghai with hundreds of events.



She began her journey with TEC in the year 2020. She represented the Youth Chapter of Shanghai Overseas Returned Scholars Association (SORSA) and co-hosted TEC's forum “Building a Better Tomorrow: Reflections on Covid-19 and Cooperation for the Future”. She has been invited to attend a number of TEC events and she appreciates the friendship with TEC members.
