Minki Chang

USA


Co-Founder, General Manager of MustardTek

Minki has graduated from Rochester Intitute of Technology with Bachelor of Science Degree in Electrical Engineering

Minki was a co-founder of 2 STEM education companies, focused on human centered design and technology to empower youth and people with disabiltiies.

Previously, Minki has 10 years of integrating innovative technology to drive business transformation at PwC and Procter and Gamble.

I feel quite blessed that there a place I can share what I have learned to make a positive impact in our local community and to inspire others to do the same. ”
