Kenneth Jarrett

USA


Senior Advisor of Albright Stonebridge Group

Kenneth currently serves as a Senior Advisor at Albright Stonebridge Group. He was also the President of American Chamber of Commerce in Shanghai from 2013 to 2018. He was also the Chairman of Greater China for APCO Worldwide, a public affairs consultancy from 2008 to 2013. Previously, he was a distinguished diplomat whose positions included service as Consul General, U.S. Consulate General, Shanghai in 2005-2008.



“It has been my pleasure to get to know TEC and offer my support. My experience has included working with Linda on plans to establish an advisory council from the diplomatic community, attending the job fair two years ago, and supporting the Christmas gala in the year 2020. Last but not least, serving as part of Linda’s informal “kitchen cabinet”, giving her advice when she needs it.”
