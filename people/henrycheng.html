Henry Cheng

Singapore


Managing Director of Durst China

Henry presently works for an Italian MNC heading their operation in Greater China Region. They are the manufacturer of advanced digital printing and production technologies with headquarters in Brixen, South Tyrol, Italy. The company’s core business is digital inkjet printing and the associated ecosystem of printing inks, software and services.  

Prior to joining this company, he was running his own business dealing with construction materials with international clients. He was based in Foshan for 10 years prior selling away his business to take up the Managing Director role in Shanghai.



Henry is active in motivating the interaction among the youth between Singapore and China. He had helped in the Youth Entrepreneurship Program where he was involved in garnering support from National Youth Council from Singapore.
