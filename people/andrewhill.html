Andrew Hill

USA


Founder, Director of ELG & Xiersen

Andrew was awarded the Charity Stars in Shanghai in the year 2010. He co-founded ELG in 2006 and founded local NGO Xiersen in 2015. He was also selected by Rotary International to be the Special Representative for the organization located in China.  



“I have enjoyed being part of the TEC community for the past few years. Our cooperation has included working on projects to support adults and children with Special needs and bringing Shanghai closer together by introducing more awareness and inclusion to activities and events. TEC has brought many leaders and local support to our mission which has been very helpful. Look forward to continuing the partnership.”
