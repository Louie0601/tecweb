Beecher Ashley Brown

Australia


CEO of AJINGA

Beecher is a Founding Partner and CEO of AJINGA. AJINGA is one of China’s leading recruitment technology providers, pioneering the use of big data, intelligent bots, candidate selection algorithms and mobile-social systems in the recruiting process.  

Prior to founding AJINGA, Beecher served as the General Manager (East China) of Hewitt Associates, China’s leading HR and Talent consulting business. In this role, Beecher had full P&L responsibility for East China managing of team of over 50 consultants in the Talent, Human Capital Analytics and Benefits consulting practices.  

Beecher graduated from Flinders University of South Australia with a Bachelor of Economics and a Bachelor of International Business. Beecher is conversant in Mandarin Chinese.



“AJINGA is proud to support TEC with recruiting technology to power the TEC recruiting website and WeChat account, as wel as its annual 3E job fair.”
