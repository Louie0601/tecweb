Carlo D’Andrea

Italy


President of DP Group/Managing Partner of D’Andrea & Partners Legal Counsel/National Vice President of European Union Chamber of Commerce in China

Graduated in Law at the University of Teramo (Italy), Carlo then subsequently obtained a Masters’ degree in Corporate Advisor at LUISS University in Rome and a Diploma in Chinese Studies at Nanjing Normal University and is a member of the Italian Bar Association.

Carlo has worked in the European Union Chamber of Commerce in China(EUCCC) since 2012, served as the chairman of European Union Chamber of Commerce in China, Nanjing Chapter from 2012 to 2014, worked in the Shanghai chapter since 2014 as the vice chairman and chairman of the Shanghai chapter until 2021. Carlo is now in the position as national vice president of the European Union Chamber of Commerce in China since May 2021.

Since August 2013, Carlo has been working as the founder and managing partner of D’Andrea & Partners Legal Counsel. After several years of development, D’Andrea & Partners Legal Counsel has set up offices in China (Shanghai, Beijing, Chongqing and Nanjing), Italy (Milan and Pescara), Vietnam (Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City), India (Pune) and the United Arab Emirates (Dubai) ,as well as Russian Desk, and has won the 2021 Le Fonti Award, 2021 “China Business Law Journal” A-List and other awards. His personal awards include: Capital Elite, “China Business Law Journal” Elite Lawyers in the Chinese Legal Market in 2021.



Carlo and his wife were invited to the annual charity gala and it was a really nice event. Besides, Carlo was invited to speak on a career fair organized in Shanghai and he met many people, many young motivated talent on the fair. “We’re here thanks to TEC, we are happy to contribute to the growth of this amazing family.”
