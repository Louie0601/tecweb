Curtis Ferguson

USA


Managing Partner of Ventech China

Curt is a managing partner at Ventech China where he leads Ventech China investing in Consumer Markets, focusing on the fast-growing Chinese Consumer Class.

Prior to that, Curt was a 38 year veteran of The Coca-Cola Company and was President of The Coca-Cola Greater China and Korea Business Unit. Curt was responsible for a US $12 billion retail business The BU operation covers more than 1.7 billion population. Across his career, he was responsible for 50+ countries.

Curt holds a Bachelor of Science degree with majors in Finance and Management from the Indiana University Kelley School. In April 2015, he was elected to the Academy of Alumni Fellows at Indiana University and is on the Board of the Indiana University Foundation.

Curt was named to Forbes Top Ten Influential Business Executives in the Middle east for 7 consensus years. Recently, he was awarded as superior corporate adviser to Bao Steel Packaging - World’s largest steel maker.



“I was introduced to TEC by Linda Painan. I feel TEC plays an essential role in the ex-pat community. Hopefully, I can bring my past experience on NGO Boards, to TEC to help build a thriving net-worked ex-patriot network that not only builds great friendships but also a caring community for those less fortunate in our welcoming host country.”
