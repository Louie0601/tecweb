ABOUT US

The Expatriate Center (TEC) is a key program of Shanghai Family Life Center – a non-profit organization legally registered and supported by the Shanghai municipal government to help expatriates Build A Home Away From Home. TEC is an one-stop service platform that offers a direct window, both offline and online, to address the actual needs of the foreigners, Chinese Hong Kong, Chinese Macao, Chinese Taiwan residents and Chinese returnees in the areas of living, working, and family in Shanghai. Furthermore, TEC also serves as a channel to provide feedback to the Shanghai government on policies and programs important to the international community.

OUR MISSION

The events and services offered by TEC are designed to help foreigners understand, cope, adapt and thrive in Shanghai as expatriate residents. Such events and services include but are not limited to Exploring Educational Opportunities (EEO) series whereby Principals and Head of Schools from International Schools are invited to be part of a panel and share about their schools directly to parents; multiple education-related events such as the International Debate Competition and the Future of Education series for educators; leisure events such as sports competitions, family days and charity events like our annual Charity Gala.


TEC also works closely with various district government bodies to provide direct consultation service on a wide range of topics, including personal tax, work and residence permit, business registration, and exit-entry policies. We are involved in key community projects like the International 3E Fair (Employment, Entrepreneurship & Enterprise). All of these activities help to build strong communities for the foreigners in Shanghai.


TEC also encourages expatriates to give back to the local community by participating in doing good works. Through TEC, an expatriate can adapt to life in Shanghai in many ways, by both receiving and giving.
