TEC 助福 Mini Program

TEC 助福 is a mini program that serves as a bilingual volunteer platform that combines resources to enable the wider community to contribute, support and do good works in Shanghai. Through connecting between individuals, corporates and organizations, TEC 助福 mini program makes it more convenient and accessible for individuals and corporates to volunteer for organizations.

BENEFITS

For Individuals

  1. Sign up volunteer projects you’re passionate about
  2. Meet like-minded people
  3. Earn your certificate and report right away

For Corporates

  1. Generate meaningful team building activities
  2. Fulfill CSR with just a click away
  3. Build up corporate culture and more branding exposure

For Organizations

  1. Set up a project and call out for volunteers
  2. Check-in easily for event volunteers on-site
  3. Raise awareness for your cause
